Instructions

Purpose

This interface helps you match talented employees with available rotation opportunities based on skills, experience, and career interests.

What it's intended for

  • Identifying the best talent matches for open roles
  • Supporting career development through strategic rotations
  • Optimizing team composition and skill distribution

What it's NOT intended for

  • Performance evaluation or disciplinary actions
  • Permanent role assignments
  • Salary or promotion decisions

Methods to provide feedback

Use the feedback button in the top right corner or contact your HRBP directly for suggestions and improvements.
